Everything Under One Roof

At HUGS, we understand how overwhelming it can be to juggle multiple appointments at different clinics. It’s such a challenge to have one for speech therapy, another for occupational support, and yet another for a doctor’s consultation. That’s why we’ve brought it all together.


From counseling to therapy to medical guidance, everything your child needs is available in one comforting space.


We’re open on weekends and all weekdays (except Fridays), making it easier for working parents to find the support they need, without the added stress of running around.


Because your child’s care should feel simple, not scattered.
