Because every child deserves to feel seen. And every parent deserves to feel supported.

At HUGS,we understand that reaching out for help isn’t always easy. Especially when it’s about your child.


You may be feeling unsure. You may have questions you don’t know how to ask. Or maybe… You just have a feeling that something’s not quite right.


Whatever brings you here, we want you to know you’re not alone. And you’re in the right place.

Who We Are

HUGS is a child development initiative by HULM, created to support children in ways that honour who they are, how they grow, and what they uniquely need. Whether your child is neurotypical or neurodivergent, we walk alongside your family with care, knowledge, and compassion.

We bring together a team of kind, skilled professionals like child psychologists, occupational therapists, speech-language specialists, special educators, paediatric neurologists, and behavioural therapists, who work collaboratively to offer personalised, holistic care for your child.

What We Believe

We believe every child deserves to be met with understanding, not assumptions. We believe early support can change the course of a child’s life. We believe parents shouldn’t have to navigate this alone.

At HUGS, we don’t rush to label. We listen. We observe. We take time to understand your child, not just their challenges, but their strengths, their joys, and the way they see the world.

A Space to Breathe

Our centre is warm, inclusive, and designed to feel safe, not just for children, but for parents too. We know how much courage it takes to walk through that door for the first time. That’s why our approach is always gentle, honest, and non-judgmental.

You don’t have to have it all figured out. That’s what we’re here for.

Your Family, Our Focus

We support the child, but we walk with the family.

Because when parents feel seen and supported, they can show up with more clarity, calm, and connection, which makes all the difference in a child’s life.

We’ll sit with you in the uncertainty.
We’ll guide you with experience.
And together, we’ll find a way forward that truly fits your child and your heart.

Let’s build a path of care, together.
