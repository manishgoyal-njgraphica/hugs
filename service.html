Helping Every Child Grow with Care, Connection, and Confidence

At HUGS, we know that every child’s journey is different, and so are their needs. That’s why we offer a wide range of personalized services that support a child’s emotional, developmental, behavioural, and learning growth at every stage.


From early screenings to skill-building therapies, our services are designed to empower not just the child but the entire family. Whether your child is neurotypical, neurodivergent, or somewhere in between, our team is here to help them thrive with the right kind of support, at the right time.

SPEECH THERAPY

When words are hard, we listen harder.


Speech therapy at HUGS is about more than articulation. It’s about helping your child be heard, in every way that matters.
Whether they’re struggling with clarity, fluency, or finding alternative ways to communicate, our therapists gently guide children through:

Speech Sound
Disorders

Helping children pronounce words more clearly, reducing frustration and building confidence in conversation.

Fluency Disorders (Stuttering Therapy)

Supporting children who stutter with evidence-based, compassionate techniques, without pressure or shame.

Oral Placement Therapy (OPT)

Focusing on muscle strength, stability, and coordination to support clear speech development.

Voice
Disorders

Addressing concerns like hoarseness, vocal fatigue, or pitch issues in a playful and supportive manner.

AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication)

For non-verbal children or those with limited speech, we introduce tools like communication boards or devices, helping them express thoughts, needs, and feelings effectively.

Occupational Therapy

Big feelings? Small frustrations? We help with both.

At HUGS, we understand that reaching out for help isn’t always easy. Especially when it’s about your child.

Occupational therapy is often misunderstood. It’s not just about ‘occupation’, it’s about giving your child the tools to engage with the world more fully, joyfully, and independently.

Through play-based and sensory-friendly approaches, we support:

  • Developmental Delays

    Identifying and addressing missed or delayed milestones with gentle, structured interventions.

  • Gross Motor Deficits

    Helping with balance, strength, posture, and coordination for better movement and confidence in physical activities.

  • Fine Motor Impairments

    Improving hand strength, writing skills, and everyday tasks like buttoning shirts or holding cutlery.

  • Sensory Processing Issues

    For kids who are easily overwhelmed by sights, sounds, textures, or movements. We help them regulate and respond in ways that feel safe and manageable.

  • Emotional Dysregulation

    Supporting children who experience big, hard-to-handle emotions by developing co-regulation skills and calming routines.

  • Behavioural Challenges

    Understanding the “why” behind tantrums, resistance, or avoidance, and building more adaptive responses through play.

  • Social Interaction Difficulties

    Building skills like turn-taking, initiating play, reading social cues, and sustaining friendships.

  • Attention & Executive Deficits

    Helping children improve focus, memory, task completion, and emotional control in a structured yet playful way.

Because being a child isn’t easy, and neither is parenting one. We’re here to make both feel lighter.

BEHAVIOURAL THERAPY

Every behaviour is a message. We help you decode it.


We know how exhausting it is when your child’s behaviour feels confusing, unpredictable, or challenging. Our behavioural therapy services are designed not to “fix” your child, but to understand them.

Our approach includes:

Managing Challenging Behaviours

Whether it’s meltdowns, defiance, aggression, or withdrawal, we gently explore triggers, patterns, and unmet needs to create better emotional and behavioural outcomes.

Social Communication Support

Helping children understand and respond to social cues, build peer relationships, and thrive in group settings.

Attention and Learning Concerns

Supporting children who struggle with sustained focus, following instructions, or adapting to school routines by helping them learn in ways that suit their style.

Developmental
Assessments

Clarity brings comfort. Early understanding creates stronger outcomes.

Not sure what your child needs? That’s okay. You’re not supposed to know everything.

Our team offers multidisciplinary assessments to help you understand your child’s developmental profile with warmth and precision. These assessments are gentle, age-appropriate, and designed to bring insight, not labels.

We assess:

  • Cognitive skills
  • Language abilities
  • Emotional regulation
  • Motor development
  • Sensory processing
  • Behavioural functioning

After the assessment, we offer a clear explanation, supportive feedback, and a roadmap for what comes next — whether that’s therapy, home strategies, or just some monitored waiting.

You’ll never leave confused, judged, or overwhelmed.

Expert
Consultations

When the path gets unclear, we bring in the right guide.

Sometimes, you may need a deeper opinion or medical perspective before taking your next step. We offer in-house and referred consultations with:

  • Pediatricians

    To rule out or confirm any underlying medical concerns that may be affecting development.

  • Pediatric Neurologists

    For more complex neurological questions, like seizures, delays, or sensory issues that don’t have a clear cause.

  • Child Psychologists

    For emotional or behavioural assessments, anxiety, mood concerns, or school-related stress.

We help facilitate these conversations with care and clarity, ensuring you're never left alone to navigate your child’s needs.

And above all... we offer this:

  • A safe space to ask questions you’ve been afraid to Google
  • A warm voice when your inner voice feels unsure
  • A reminder that needing help doesn't mean you're failing, it means you're showing up

At HUGS, you’re not a ‘case.’ You’re a parent. A caregiver. A human. And your child is not a ‘delay.’ They’re a full, beautiful work-in-progress.
